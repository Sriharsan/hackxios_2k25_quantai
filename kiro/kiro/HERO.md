# HERO - Institutional AI Portfolio Manager

## What We Built
An AI-powered portfolio management system that creates cryptographic proof of every investment decision using blockchain technology. Think BlackRock meets blockchain - institutional-grade analytics with unbreakable audit trails.

## The Big Problem We're Solving
Portfolio managers handle trillions in assets, but clients have zero way to verify decisions weren't changed after the fact. This trust gap costs the industry billions in compliance and creates massive legal risks. We're the first to solve this with math instead of promises.

## How Kiro Made This Possible

### Planning That Actually Worked
Kiro helped us think through this complex problem systematically instead of just diving into code. We used it to map out the institutional requirements, figure out where blockchain actually adds value (spoiler: not everywhere), and plan a realistic timeline for the hackathon.

### Smart Architecture Decisions  
The biggest win was using Kiro to think through blockchain integration. Instead of putting everything on-chain (expensive and slow), we designed a hybrid approach - portfolio data stays private, but cryptographic hashes go on Ethereum for proof. This gives institutions the trust they need without breaking the bank.

### Real-Time Problem Solving
When we hit roadblocks during development, Kiro helped us prioritize fixes and make smart trade-offs. Like when our blockchain transactions were taking too long - we used Kiro's decision framework to implement async processing instead of blocking the UI.

## What Makes This Project Stand Out

### 1. Solves a Real $2 Trillion Problem
This isn't a toy project. Institutional portfolio management is massive, and the trust/verification problem is costing real money. We're addressing genuine pain points that pension funds, endowments, and asset managers face every day.

### 2. Blockchain That Makes Sense
We didn't just add blockchain because it's trendy. Our hash anchoring approach provides mathematical proof of portfolio integrity while keeping costs under $1 per transaction. It's practical blockchain usage that institutions can actually adopt.

### 3. Production-Ready Architecture
Built with real deployment in mind. The system uses proven technologies (Python, Streamlit, Ethereum) and is designed to scale on AWS. This isn't just a hackathon demo - it's a foundation for a real business.

### 4. Institutional-Grade Features
- Advanced portfolio optimization (Markowitz, Risk Parity)
- Comprehensive risk analytics (VaR, stress testing)
- Regulatory compliance reporting
- Cryptographic audit trails
- Independent verification system
